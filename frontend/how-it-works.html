How TrustLend works

From phone number to funded loan — in under 90 seconds.

THE LOAN FLOW

5 steps. No bank required.

01

📱 Apply with your phone number

No Aadhaar card, no bank statement, no documents needed. Just enter your mobile number. Our app securely analyses your phone usage behaviour using federated learning — your data never leaves your device.

02

🤖 AI calculates your TrustScore

Our AI model analyses 12+ behavioural signals including UPI transaction history, location stability, call frequency patterns, app usage behaviour, and peer vouching network. This produces a TrustScore from 0–100 in seconds.

03

🔗 Lender matches automatically

Your loan request goes live on the marketplace. Lenders browse AI-verified borrowers and fund directly. The system matches based on risk tolerance and yield expectations. No bank involved at any step.

04

⛓️ Smart contract fires on Ethereum Sepolia

A Solidity smart contract is deployed on Ethereum Sepolia testnet. It locks the lender's funds, releases them to the borrower, and automatically handles EMI repayment — fully transparent on blockchain, zero human intervention.

05

💸 Repay to boost your score

Repay on time and your TrustScore increases. This unlocks larger loans at lower interest rates. Each repayment is recorded immutably on blockchain — building your decentralised credit history forever.

PRIVACY

Your data never leaves your phone.

TrustLend uses federated machine learning. Your raw phone data stays on your device. Only the computed TrustScore — a single number — is shared with the platform. We never see your call logs, UPI history, or personal data.
